About Me

Hi, I'm Curtis

I’m passionate about photography, but even more passionate about people. Whether I’m out on a motorcycle ride, exploring the outdoors, capturing the lines of a classic car, or just enjoying good company, I find joy in connections. Photography has become my way of preserving those moments — not just the scene, but the feeling that comes with it.

My work spans portraits, landscapes, vehicles, and product photography, but at its core, it’s about storytelling. Each image is a reflection of the adventure, the personality, or the story behind it. Through my lens, I aim to share experiences that resonate beyond the frame.
